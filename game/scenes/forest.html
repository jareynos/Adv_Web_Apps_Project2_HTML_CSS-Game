The Whispering Forest

The forest canopy muffles the world in a strange hush. The trees lean inward as though listening — or whispering — to one another.

Soft silver dust clings to fallen leaves, shimmering faintly. It matches the color of the fallen star’s trail.

Two paths lie before you:

  • Follow the tracks leading to a rushing river
  • Investigate the eerie glow deeper in the forest
